REQ-ID:JO1
Description:The website stores Holidaymaker's contact details.Contact details would be email address, mobile number and address.
Type:Functional Requirement
Rationale:To inform potential holidaymaker of available holiday let properties throughout the year,by contacting holidaymaker via email, text message to mobile or by sending out pamphlets. The originator for obtaining this information could be a database on the ‘Every Day’ website or ‘Everyday’ letting agency.
Originator:Jo Owens 

REQ-ID: JO2
Description:Creating a website that shows, a variety of profession photographs, of Landbnb’s holiday let properties, that show the types of property on offer, if they cater for a family or not,do they require off road parking, location shots. Also showing local attractions and amenities. The originator of this information would be from the property owner and letting agency representative. 
Type:Functional Requirement
Rationale: Creating a website that entices holidaymakers to make a booking. 
Orginator:Jo Owens

REQ-ID: DT2
Description: The product shall allow for booking cancellation or amendment if a natural phenomenon, or disaster affects the property
Type: Functional Requirement
