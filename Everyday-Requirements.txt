HE1
Description: The product shall accept the contact details of the holidaymaker 
Type: Functional Requirement
Rationale: This information is required to enable a booking and booking confirmation to be made/sent
Originator: Ayisha Jayakodi

HE2
Description: The product shall accept the details of the property requirements (kind, price, dates, location) of the holidaymaker
Type: Functional Requirement
Rationale: This information is required to enable a suitable list of available properties to be provided to allow holidaymakers to make a choice for a rental booking and/or visit
Originator: Ayisha Jayakodi

HE3
Description: The product shall produce a list of available holiday properties based on the holidaymaker's property requirements
Type: Functional Requirement
Rationale: To enable the holidaymaker to have a list of potential properties which are suitable for them to make a choice for a possible rental
Originator: Ayisha Jayakodi

HE4
The product shall accept a property visit request from a prospective holidaymaker
Type: Functional Requirement
Rationale: To enable a visit to a potential rental property to be arranged for the holidaymaker
Originator: Ayisha Jayakodi

AB1
The product shall accept the booking details of a property from a holidaymaker
Type: Functional Requirement
Rationale: To confirm that the property is available, update that property's availability, enable production of a booking and determine the rental charge
Originator: Ayisha Jayakodi

AB2
The product shall check the date availability of a property for a proposed booking
Type: Functional Requirement
Rationale: To confirm that the property is available for the requested booking
Originator: Ayisha Jayakodi

AB3
The product shall accept any special arrangements for a booking from a holidaymaker
Type: Functional Requirement
Rationale: To ensure that those specific arrangements can be made for the particular holiday rental period of the booking
Originator: Ayisha Jayakodi

AB4
The product shall accept the payment for a holidaymaker’s booking
Type: Functional Requirement
Rationale: To enable payment for the holiday and confirmation of the rental
Originator: Ayisha Jayakodi

AB5
The product shall produce a booking confirmation for a holidaymaker’s booking
Type: Functional Requirement
Rationale: To provide confirmation information to both holidaymaker and property owner
Originator: Ayisha Jayakodi

ROP1
The product shall accept the Property Owner’s contact details
Type: Functional Requirement
Rationale: To enable production of a rental contract with, to enable any rental confirmation to be send to, the property owner
Originator: Ayisha Jayakodi

ROP2
The product shall accept the Owner's property details (photos, rooms, heating, available services, provided facilities, property features)
Type: Functional Requirement
Rationale: To enable the individual properties to be advertised by the product and to enable matching of the property with individual holidaymaker's enquiry details
Originator: Ayisha Jayakodi

ROP3
The product shall accept a property’s rental price details.
Type: Functional Requirement
Rationale: To enable matching of the property with individual holiday maker's enquiry details; for the calculation of the rental charge for a booking; to use this information in the Owner's rental contract.
Originator: Ayisha Jayakodi

ROP4
The product shall produce a rental contract for an Owner’s property
Type: Functional Requirement
Rationale: To enable a signed binding contract to be made between Landbnb and property owner.
Originator: Ayisha Jayakodi

OIA1
The product shall accept the dates of an absence from a Property Owner
Type: Functional Requirement
Rationale: To enable the production of weekly schedules for the management of individual properties.
Originator: Ayisha Jayakodi

OIA2
The product shall accept cleaning/maintenance requests from a Property Owner
Type: Functional Requirement
Rationale: To enable the production of weekly schedules for the cleaning/maintenance of individual properties.
Originator: Ayisha Jayakodi

OIA3
The product shall make up a weekly schedule of property visits for rental preparation
Type: Functional Requirement
Rationale: To enable a visit to be made to a property to prepare it for an upcoming rental
Originator: Ayisha Jayakodi

OIA4
The product shall make up a weekly schedule of maintenance/cleaning requirements for local Property Services Company
Type: Functional Requirement
Rationale: To enable requests to be made for maintenance/cleaning with the local Property Services Company to service individual properties as required.
Originator: Ayisha Jayakodi
